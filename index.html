IndicTrans2 ONNX Browser Translation

IndicTrans2 is a state-of-the-art translation model supporting English and 22 Indian languages. Run optimized ONNX models locally in your browser using WebGPU or WASM.

For detailed info, model conversion pipelines, and export scripts, please check our main GitHub Repository.

Warning: WebGPU Precision Limitation

Running INT8 or Q4F16 quantized models on WebGPU frequently causes outputs to fail or degenerate into gibberish due to browser shader/driver bugs. WASM is strongly recommended for quantized tiers.

Estimated download size: ~210 MB

Note: Since the models run entirely client-side, the weights must be downloaded and cached in your browser storage. First-time loading may take some time depending on your connection. Subsequent loads are instant.
